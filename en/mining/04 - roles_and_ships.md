| Tier | Gas yield | Ice yield | Gap | Gas cumulative | Ice cumulative |

| T1 | 48 | 60 | Ice +25% | 4h53 | 5h33 |

| T4 | 160 | 192 | Ice +20% | 3d06h | 3d15h |
| T5 | 240 | 288 | Ice +20% | 42d07h | 22d03h |

Ice is consistently more profitable in raw yield at every equivalent tier, and also faster to progress вЂ” ice endgame (T5) is reached twice as fast as gas endgame (23d vs 44d). Ice's advantage comes from both superior module base values (15/24/36 vs 12/20/30) and the absence of the Mining L45 bottleneck that gas requires. Gas partially compensates through higher unit value of endgame resources (Neurotoxin Cloud 150 cr vs Dark Glitter 100 cr).
