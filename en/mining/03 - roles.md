# Roles and Tiers — Mining System

> Tiers are defined by belt access requirements and/or the ability to mine the resources within them.

---

## 🪨 Base Miner

A role common to all miners. No specialization, no specific requirements beyond Mining. The goal is to explore the first belts and accumulate enough experience to specialize at Mining L15.

---

### Tier 1 — Beginner *(Mining L0)*

**Belt:** Rookie Fields (×1.0)

| Ore | Share | Value | Access |
|-----|:-----:|:-----:|:------:|
| Ferrite 🟤 | 70% | 5 cr | L0 |
| Copite 🟠 | 30% | 8 cr | L5 ⛔ |

The only ore available at the start is Ferrite. Copite is present but inaccessible until Mining L5. Ideal belt to learn basic mechanics.

---

### Tier 2 — Apprentice *(Mining L5)*

**Belts:** Rookie Fields + Frontier Belt (×1.1)

| Ore | Share | Value | Access | Belt |
|-----|:-----:|:-----:|:------:|------|
| Ferrite 🟤 | 40% | 5 cr | L0 | Frontier Belt |
| Copite 🟠 | 35% | 8 cr | L5 ✅ | Frontier Belt |
| Luminite 🟡 | 25% | 15 cr | L10 ⛔ | Frontier Belt |

Frontier Belt becomes accessible. Copite can now be mined. Luminite is visible but still out of reach — first glimpse of an uncommon ore.

---

### Tier 3 — Confirmed *(Mining L10)*

**Belts:** Rookie Fields + Frontier Belt (×1.1)

| Ore | Share | Value | Access | Belt |
|-----|:-----:|:-----:|:------:|------|
| Ferrite 🟤 | 40% | 5 cr | L0 | Frontier Belt |
| Copite 🟠 | 35% | 8 cr | L5 | Frontier Belt |
| Luminite 🟡 | 25% | 15 cr | L10 ✅ | Frontier Belt |

No new belt, but Luminite becomes mineable. First uncommon ore, first significant income increase. Crystal Caverns is on the horizon (Mining L15).

> At this stage, the player must choose a specialization: **Ore**, **Gas**, or **Ice**. All three require Mining L15 as an entry point.

---

## ⛏️ Ore Miner

Specialization focused on solid ores. Progression is entirely driven by Mining level — each tier unlocks either a new belt or a new mineable resource in an already accessible belt.

---

### Tier 1 — Crystal Caverns *(Mining L15)*

**Belt:** Crystal Caverns (×1.2, rich)

| Ore | Share | Value | Access |
|-----|:-----:|:-----:|:------:|
| Copite 🟠 | 20% | 8 cr | L5 |
| Luminite 🟡 | 30% | 15 cr | L10 |
| Crystite 💎 | 35% | 25 cr | L15 ✅ |
| Titanite ⚪ | 15% | 40 cr | L20 ⛔ |

First rich belt with ×1.2 multiplier. Crystite is the first notable gain. Titanite is present but inaccessible — intentional frustration.
