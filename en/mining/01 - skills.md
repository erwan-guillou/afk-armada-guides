## 1. Core Skills (ore)

---

### Mining
**Category:** Mining & Gathering | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** none

The foundational skill. Increases overall yield by **+1% per level** across all resource types (ore, gas, ice). It is also the gateway to the entire mining system — each level unlocks new belts and new ores.

**Belt and ore access by level:**

| Level | Unlocks |
|:-----:|---------|
| L0 | Rookie Fields — Ferrite |
| L5 | Frontier Belt (×1.1) — Copite |
| L10 | Luminite (in Frontier Belt) |
| L15 | Crystal Caverns (×1.2) — Crystite + access to gas and ice belts |
| L20 | Titanium Ridge (×1.3) — Titanite |
| L25 | Cobaltine (in Titanium Ridge) |
| L30 | Deep Core Sector (×1.5) — Iridite |
| L40 | Void Rift Alpha (×2.0, clan) — Voidstone |
| L45 | Toxic Maelstrom gas (×1.6, clan) |
| L50 | Starium Nebula (×2.5, clan) — Starium + Unicornium |

**Prerequisites for other skills:** Survey (L2), Mining Turrets (L3), Mining Ship Specialization (L3), Gas Harvesting (L3), Ice Harvesting (L3), Deep Core Mining (L4), Mining Automation (L5), Signature Masking (L5), Mining Drone Operation (L2), Mining Amplification (L10).

---

### Survey
**Category:** Mining & Gathering | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** Mining L2

Improves asteroid analysis, increasing overall yield by **+1% per level**. Works in synergy with equipped Survey Scanners — each level of Survey is added to the levels provided by the module to form a combined bonus.

**Prerequisites for:** Survey Upgrades (L5), Prospecting (L3), Survey Scanner I (L5).

---

### Survey Upgrades
**Category:** Mining & Gathering | **Max:** L5 | **Prerequisites:** Survey L5

Amplifies the effectiveness of equipped Survey Scanners by **+5% per level**, up to +25% at L5. This bonus applies to the levels provided by the module, not to the player's Survey skill level — it makes scanners progressively more powerful without affecting the Survey skill itself.

**Prerequisites for:** Survey Scanner II (L3).

---

### Mining Turrets
**Category:** Mining & Gathering | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** Mining L3

Increases the efficiency of all extraction modules by **+2% per level** — this includes Mining Lasers (ore), Gas Siphons, and Ice Harvesters. This is the skill governing the progression of extraction modules, whose prerequisites increase tier by tier.

**Prerequisites for:** Mining Laser I (L1), Mining Laser II (L3), Mining Laser III (L5), Gas Siphon I (L5), Gas Siphon II (L12), Gas Siphon III (L20), Ice Harvester I (L8), Ice Harvester II (L18), Ice Harvester III (L28), Titan Drill (L25).

---

### Mining Ship Specialization
**Category:** Mining & Gathering | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** Mining L3

Increases the ship's mining multiplier by **+2% per level**. This is the most profitable skill per level for mining ships, as it directly amplifies the ship's `mining_multiplier`, which is itself a multiplicative factor on the entire yield.

**Prerequisites for:** Mining Barge (L8), Extractor (L8), Ironclad (L5), Bastion (L12), Heavy Mining Barge (L28), Titan Drill (L35), Gas Mining Specialization (L5), Ice Mining Specialization (L5).

---

### Deep Core Mining
**Category:** Mining & Gathering | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** Mining L4

Increases the chance of obtaining rare ores in belts by **+2% per level**. Higher-tier belts have higher base rates, making this skill progressively more useful as the player accesses deeper areas.

---

### Prospecting
**Category:** Mining & Gathering | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** Survey L3

Adds **+1% yield on rare and legendary ores** per level. A natural complement to Deep Core Mining — where Deep Core Mining increases the chance of finding a rare, Prospecting increases the quantity recovered when that happens.

---

### Mining Amplification
**Category:** Mining & Gathering | **Max:** L5 | **Prerequisites:** Mining L10

Unlocks and amplifies Mining Yield Amplifiers (low-slot modules). Without this skill, amplifiers cannot be equipped. Each level increases their efficiency by **+2%**, but the main impact is the progressive unlocking of tier I (L1) and tier II (L4).

---

## 2. Gas Specialization

---

### Gas Harvesting
**Category:** Mining & Gathering | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** Mining L3

Unlocks access to gas belts and increases gas yield by **+5% per level** — the most powerful resource multiplier in the game. At L50, it multiplies gas yield by ×3.5 compared to a player without this skill. The base prerequisite for the entire gas branch.

**Access by level:**

| Level | Unlocks |
|:-----:|---------|
| L1 | Fullerite Pocket (×0.9) — Fullerite Gas |
| L5 | Gas Siphon I + Gas Ship Command (with Industrial L5) |
| L10 | Gas Mining Specialization + Vapor Collector |
| L12 | Gas Siphon II |
| L20 | Gas Siphon III + Mining Leviathan |

---

### Gas Mining Specialization
**Category:** Mining & Gathering | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** Gas Harvesting L10 + Mining Ship Specialization L5

Increases gas yield by **+2% per level**, in addition to Gas Harvesting. Prerequisite for the Mining Leviathan (L10), the endgame gas ship.

---

## 3. Ice Specialization

---

### Ice Harvesting
**Category:** Mining & Gathering | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** Mining L3

Unlocks access to ice belts and increases ice yield by **+5% per level** — symmetric to Gas Harvesting. At L50, multiplies ice yield by ×3.5. The base prerequisite for the entire ice branch.

**Access by level:**

| Level | Unlocks |
|:-----:|---------|
| L1 | Frozen Expanse (×1.0) — Clear Ice |
| L5 | Ice Harvester I + Ice Ship Command (with Industrial L5) |
| L10 | Ice Mining Specialization + Frostbreaker |
| L18 | Ice Harvester II (via Mining Turrets L18) |
| L20 | Glacier King |

---

### Ice Mining Specialization
**Category:** Mining & Gathering | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** Ice Harvesting L10 + Mining Ship Specialization L5

Increases ice yield by **+2% per level**, in addition to Ice Harvesting. Prerequisite for Ice Harvesters II (L15) and III (L30), and for the Glacier King (L10).

---

## 4. Ship Command

---

### Shuttle Piloting
**Category:** Spaceship Command | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** none

The basic piloting skill. Increases Starter Shuttle stats by **+1% per level**. Essential as a prerequisite for Industrial Command (L3), which is itself the gateway to all progression toward barges and haulers.

**Prerequisites for:** Industrial Command (L3).

---

### Industrial Command
**Category:** Spaceship Command | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** Shuttle Piloting L3

Unlocks Mining Barges and Haulers, and increases their stats by **+1% per level**. This is the central skill of mining progression — almost all advanced industrial ships require it at high levels.

**Ship access:**

| Level | Ship |
|:-----:|------|
| L1 | Prospector |
| L5 | Mining Skiff + Gas Ship Command + Ice Ship Command |
| L8 | Vapor Collector + Frostbreaker |
| L10 | Ironclad + Extractor |
| L12 | Mining Barge |
| L15 | Glacier King + Mining Leviathan + Oremaster |
| L18 | Bastion |
| L30 | Heavy Mining Barge |
| L35 | Titan Drill |

---

### Gas Ship Command
**Category:** Spaceship Command | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** Gas Harvesting L5 + Industrial Command L5

Unlocks dedicated gas ships and increases their stats by **+2% per level**. The command multiplier applies to the global ship multiplier, amplifying the entire yield.

| Level | Ship |
|:-----:|------|
| L1 | Nebula Runner (gas×2.0) |
| L2 | Vapor Collector (gas×2.5) |
| L3 | Mining Leviathan (gas×4.0) |

---

### Ice Ship Command
**Category:** Spaceship Command | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** Ice Harvesting L5 + Industrial Command L5

Unlocks dedicated ice ships and increases their stats by **+2% per level**. Symmetric to Gas Ship Command.

| Level | Ship |
|:-----:|------|
| L1 | Ice Skimmer (ice×2.0) |
| L2 | Frostbreaker (ice×2.5) |
| L3 | Glacier King (ice×4.0) |

## 5. Mining Drones

---

### Mining Drone Operation
**Category:** Mining & Gathering | **Max:** L10 | **Prerequisites:** Mining L2 + Drone Control L1

Unlocks and increases the number of mining drones deployable simultaneously by **+1 per level**, up to 10 drones at L10. Without this skill, no mining drones can be used. Prerequisite for Mining Foreman Link I (L5).

---

### Drone Mining Yield
**Category:** Drones | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** Mining Automation L5

Increases the yield of each mining drone by **+2% per level**. Stacks across all simultaneously deployed drones — with 5 drones and this skill at L10, the gain is significant.

---

### Drone Mining Efficiency
**Category:** Drones | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** Mining Automation L5

Reduces mining drone cycle time by **-1% per level**, increasing their extraction rate. Works as a complement to Drone Mining Yield — one increases yield per cycle, the other increases the number of cycles per minute.

---

## 6. Cycle Optimization

---

### Mining Automation
**Category:** Mining & Gathering | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** Mining L5

Reduces mining cycle time by **-0.5% per level**. The most important milestone is **L10: unlocks auto-cycle** — the ship automatically unloads and resumes mining without player intervention, radically transforming session efficiency.

**Prerequisites for:** Drone Mining Yield (L5), Drone Mining Efficiency (L5).

---

### Navigation
**Category:** Navigation | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** none

Increases ship movement speed by **+1% per level**. In mining with auto-cycle, ship speed directly determines unloading travel time between the belt and the station — a faster ship spends less time moving and more time mining.

**Prerequisites for:** Reaction Drive Injector I (L1), Reaction Drive Injector II (L2), Emergency Warp Efficiency (L3), Signature Masking (indirectly).

---

### Cargo Optimization
**Category:** Engineering | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** CPU Management L2

Increases ship cargo capacity by **+2% per level**. In auto-cycle, a larger cargo hold means more ore per trip and therefore proportionally less time spent traveling. Prerequisite for Cargo Expanders II (L3) and III (L5).

---

### Hull Upgrades
**Category:** Engineering | **Max:** L10 | **Prerequisites:** CPU Management L2

Increases hull HP by **+1% per level**. Relevant in mining primarily as a prerequisite for speed modules: Mass-Reduced Frame I (L1) and II (L2).

---

### Shield Management
**Category:** Engineering | **Max:** L10 | **Prerequisites:** Power Grid Management L2

Increases shield regeneration by **+1% per level**. Relevant in mining as a prerequisite for the Ironclad (L8) and Bastion (L10) ships — two barges focused on survival.

---

### Defense Systems
**Category:** Combat | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** none

Increases shield and hull HP by **+1% per level**. Relevant in mining only as a prerequisite for the Bastion (L10), the endgame defensive barge.

---

## 7. Fleet & Command

---

### Mining Foreman
**Category:** Mining & Gathering | **Max:** L5 | **Prerequisites:** Industrial Command L10

Increases the mining yield of the entire allied fleet by **+1% per level** and unlocks fleet command modules. This is the pivotal skill for the mining fleet commander role.

**Prerequisites for:** Cargo Coordinator I/II/III, Mining Link I/II/III, Oremaster, Mining Foreman Link II.

---

## 8. Deep Space Survival

These skills do not directly affect yield but are critical for miners operating in deep space, where hunter presence is a constant threat.

---

### Signature Masking
**Category:** Navigation | **Max:** L50 | **Prerequisites:** Mining L5

Reduces the growth of the ship's sensor signature by **-1% per level** with each asteroid mined. In deep space, each mining session increases the ship's signature, making it easier for hunters to detect — this skill slows that progression.

---

### Emergency Warp Efficiency
**Category:** Navigation | **Max:** L20 | **Prerequisites:** Navigation L3

Reduces the amount of cargo lost when fleeing by **-2% per level**. The base rate is 50% of cargo lost on escape — at L20, this drops to a minimum of 10%. Essential for miners operating in dangerous areas with a full cargo hold.

---

## 9. Cross-cutting Skills

These skills have no direct effect on yield or cycle time, but allow equipping more modules or more powerful modules that consume more fitting resources.

---

### CPU Management
**Category:** Engineering | **Max:** L10 | **Prerequisites:** none

Increases ship CPU capacity by **+1% per level**. Some mining modules consume CPU — higher CPU allows fitting more of them, or accessing higher-tier modules with greater consumption.

---

### Power Grid Management
**Category:** Engineering | **Max:** L10 | **Prerequisites:** none

Increases ship Power Grid capacity by **+1% per level**. Extraction and mining support modules consume Power Grid — higher PG offers the same fitting flexibility as CPU Management.
